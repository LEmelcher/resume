Research Assistant

Aug 2019 - present
Graduate Research Assistant at Rochester Institute of Technology, NY

My current role is a Graduate Research Assistant at the Rochester Institute of Technology.

My research consists of investigating colloidal networks as a model system that can dynamically switch between crosslinked and unlinked states via crosslinkers.

We also use Brownian Dynamics simulations and analyzing properties, such as the degree order in the system, connected clusters size, and the mechanical rigidity of the system as a function of time for different volume fractions of colloids and crosslinking kinetics.

Our results can provide insights into the design of self-sustaining soft materials that can autonomously transition between solid-like and uid-like states, and how the properties of such materials can be tuned.
